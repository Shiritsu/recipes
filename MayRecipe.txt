This is a recipe for a cake:

2.5 cups of regular flour
2 bags of baking powder
2 bags of vanilla suger
2 cups of white sugar
3 Tbps coco powder
3 eggs 
1 cup oil
2 cups of boiling water

How to:
1. mix all the dry ingredians
2. add eggs and oil and mix
3. add boiling water and mix
4. bake at 150C for 40 mintutes
